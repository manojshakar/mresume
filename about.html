About
Hello, I'm Manoj Shakar, a Self-taught product designer who is passionate about creating beautiful and functional user-friendly products. Currently, crafting user experience at Freshworks, previously at Trimble.

My passion gives me the desire to excel and continuously improve in my line of work. I identify the user problems and solve them by providing a better experience that is as aesthetically pleasing as functional.

I also love (re)creating brand logos that would be far more meaningful to their target audience and the philosophy of the company.

When I am not at my desk, you can always find me traveling to new places, meeting new people, and capturing moments of my life with my camera.
