What is Journey builder?
Journey Builder is a feature within Freshmarketer, a comprehensive marketing automation platform. It allows businesses to create and automate personalized customer journeys based on specific triggers, actions, and conditions. With Journey Builder, marketers can deliver targeted and relevant messages to customers at different stages of their journey, increasing engagement, conversions, and overall customer satisfaction.
Use case for Journey builder: Welcome Email Series
Trigger: A new subscriber signs up for a newsletter on a website.

Condition: The first action in the journey is to send an automated welcome email to the subscriber, expressing gratitude for joining and providing a brief introduction to the brand or company.

Action 1: If the subscriber opens the welcome email, they receive a follow-up email after a specified time interval with additional information about the brand's products or services.

Action 2: If the subscriber doesn't open the welcome email, a reminder email is triggered after a specific time period, encouraging them to engage with the content.

The goal of the journey is to engage the subscriber from the start, provide valuable information about the brand, and nurture them towards becoming an active and engaged member of the newsletter community.

User research

We conducted user research by sending a survey to our customers, gathering valuable feedback on their experience. Based on their input, we identified and prioritized the major issues. We then worked diligently to address these concerns and make improvements to enhance the overall user experience.
Problem + Opportunity


Here are a few challenges that users may face while using Journey builder:
Lack of visual hierarchy and no modern UI:
One problem with the Journey Builder is the lack of a clear visual hierarchy and a modern user interface (UI). The absence of a visually appealing design and organised layout can make it challenging for users to quickly understand the flow of their journeys. A cluttered interface with inconsistent or outdated visuals may lead to confusion and hinder the user experience.


Discoverability issue in node connection:
Another problem is the lack of discoverability when connecting nodes in the Journey Builder. Users may find it difficult to locate and establish connections between different actions, conditions, and triggers. This can result in frustration and potential errors in creating the desired flow for the campaign.
Lack of intuitiveness and affordance in action and condition blocks:
The action and condition blocks within the Journey Builder may lack intuitiveness and affordance, making it challenging for users to understand their functionalities at a glance. Users may have to rely on trial and error or consult documentation to fully comprehend the options and settings available for each block.
Goal

Modern UI:
The Journey Builder could benefit from a redesign that incorporates modern UI principles, such as a clean and intuitive layout, clear visual indicators for different elements, and a logical hierarchy that guides users through the journey creation process. This would enhance usability and make it easier for users to navigate and comprehend their campaigns.
Improving Node Connection in the Journey Builder:
The Journey Builder could introduce more prominent visual cues or highlighting mechanisms to indicate the available connection points and streamline the process of linking nodes. Clearer labels, tooltips, or drag-and-drop functionality could make it easier for users to establish connections between various elements within the journey.
Enhancing Usability of Action and Condition Blocks in the Journey Builder:
The Journey Builder could provide more descriptive labels, contextual help, and interactive elements within the action and condition blocks. Clear instructions, tooltips, and examples can guide users in setting up actions and conditions effectively.
