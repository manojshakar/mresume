What is social media campaign?
A social media campaign refers to a coordinated marketing or promotional effort that utilises social media platforms to achieve specific goals. It involves creating and sharing content on social media channels to engage with a target audience, build brand awareness, drive website traffic, generate leads, or promote a product, service, or cause.
What is Journey builder?
Journey Builder is a feature within Freshmarketer, a comprehensive marketing automation platform. It allows businesses to create and automate personalized customer journeys based on specific triggers, actions, and conditions. With Journey Builder, marketers can deliver targeted and relevant messages to customers at different stages of their journey, increasing engagement, conversions, and overall customer satisfaction.
Use case for Journey builder: Welcome Email Series

UX Strategy
  • Create user personas and user journeys to represent target users and their interactions with the product.
  • Define the product's value proposition and core features.

Ideation
  • Generate a range of design ideas and concepts.
  • Sketch or create site map and user flow for the feature.

Wireframe
  • Sketch or create wireframes to visualise the user interface (UI) and interaction flow.
  • Conduct brainstorming sessions and collaborate with the team to refine and iterate on design concepts.

Design
  • Design consistent layouts, typography, colours, icons, and other visual elements.
  • Ensure the UI is intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing.

User story

As a digital marketing manager at Dunzo Daily, Prem wants to run a targeted social media campaign to increase brand awareness and drive customer engagement. He needs a user-friendly platform that allows him to create and schedule captivating posts, track campaign performance metrics, and manage multiple social media platforms seamlessly. By utilizing a comprehensive social media campaign tool, Prem aims to optimize his marketing efforts and achieve impactful results for Dunzo Daily.

Learnings
In the process of creating a social media campaign tool, there is a continuous learning curve that involves understanding user needs, staying updated with evolving social media platforms, addressing technical challenges, and incorporating user feedback to enhance the tool's functionality and usability. It requires a proactive approach to adapt to changes in social media trends and technologies, ensuring the tool remains relevant and valuable for marketers in optimising their social media campaigns.
